About the School

This holy temple of Devi Saraswati was established on 22 March 1926. Thompson Inter College Gonda, the leading school of Devipatan Mandal, presentsly "Shaheed-e-Azam Sardar Bhagat Singh Inter College Gonda" emerged as "Aman Sabha High School" subsequently. Thompson Intermediate College Trust Fund Gonda Charitable Endowment Act 1890 under Gazette No-127/G//XV-414 published on 24-02-1927 Government Gazette U.P. part 01 dated 24-02-1927 named this school as Thompson Inter College Gonda. This school school was named after the then District Magistrate Mr. S.H. Thompson made a commendable contribution in the development of this institution. In the year 1991, according to the public sentiment, the school was named as Shaheed-e-Azam Sardar Bhagat Singh Inter College Gonda by the state government.
The Late Khawaja Masood Ali has the distinction of being the first principal of this college, Located on the Lucknow highway. Thereafter due to the continuous efforts of late R P Srivastava for about 26 years, The college got the excellent position in the state. This school has provided the nation with qualified administrators, doctors, engineers, eminent teachers, eminent judges and advocates as well as intelligent citizens.

location and extent of the college

This college is located in the Civil Lines Kachehari Road Gonda headquarters. It is surrounded by the LBS PG College to the East, The irrigation department in the west, the science faculty of LBS PG College In the south and Lucknow road in the north. It is spread over an area of about 25 acres.
